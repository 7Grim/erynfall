## SKILL 5: PRAYER

**Core Mechanic:** Gain XP by burying bones; unlock prayers (buffs) with levels

### Bone Types & XP (Tied to Enemy Difficulty)

| Bone Type | Level | XP per Bury | Source |
|-----------|-------|------------|--------|
| Bones | 1 | 4.5 | Chickens, rats, goblins |
| Big bones | 15 | 15 | Skeletons, guards |
| Baby dragon bones | 30 | 30 | Baby dragons, imps |
| Dragon bones | 40 | 72 | Adult dragons |
| Dagannoths bones | 50 | 125 | Dagannoth enemies |
| Ourg bones | 60 | 140 | Ourg Cults, giants |
| Superiore dragon bones | 70+ | 200+ | Wyverns, superior mobs |

**Key Mechanic:** Harder enemies = better bones = faster Prayer XP. This incentivizes training combat first, then Prayer.

### Prayer Points System

**Max Prayer Points = Prayer Level**
- Level 1 Prayer = 1 max PP
- Level 50 Prayer = 50 max PP
- Level 99 Prayer = 99 max PP

**Restoration Methods:**
- Prayer at altar: Instantly restore to max PP (found throughout world)
- Prayer potion: Restore (Prayer level × 0.25) + 7 PP per dose
- Example: Level 50 Prayer = +19 PP per dose

| 80 | Raw Shark | Shark | 210 | 20 | 1% | Extreme |
| 90+ | Raw Anglerfish | Anglerfish | 230 | 22 | 1% | Extreme |

**Burn Rate Mechanic:** Higher Cooking level = fewer burns. At level 99, burns are nearly impossible.

### XP Gain
- **Per food cooked:** 10-230 XP (varies by food)
- Can burn food (wasted resources, no XP for burnt food)
- Cumulative XP to level 99: 13,034,431 XP

### Profit Potential
- Cooked food value: Often 2-5× higher than raw
- Level 30-60: 30k-100k gp/hour (high profit)
- Level 80+: 100k-300k gp/hour (extreme profit)

### Training Method (MVP)
1. Obtain raw fish from Fishing
2. Use raw fish on range/fire
3. Wait for cook to complete
4. Bank and repeat
- Very AFK (3-5 second cook times)
- Can burn food if cooking high-level food at low skill
- **Economy link:** Cooked food → Combat players (essential for healing)

### Equipment
- **Range:** Faster, higher success rate
- **Fire:** Slower, lower success rate, cheaper alternative
- **Gauntlets:** Cooking gauntlets reduce burn rate by 10-20%

### Supply/Demand
- Combat players constantly need food
- Direct demand from all players grinding Combat, Magic, etc.
- Creates premium price for high-level cooked food
- Economic cornerstone (enables all combat training profitably)

## INTERCONNECTED ECONOMY (MVP CRITICAL)

### Supply Chain Flow

```
Woodcutting (Logs harvested)
  ├→ Supply for Fletching (future) - arrows, bows
  ├→ Sell raw to GE - profit
  └→ Use in Firemaking (future)

Fishing (Fish harvested)
  ├→ Sell raw to GE - low profit
  ├→ Supply for Cooking - transform to high profit
  └→ Can be eaten raw (no healing, not recommended)

Cooking (Fish processed)
  ├→ Sell cooked to GE - high profit
  ├→ Use in Combat training - ESSENTIAL for healing
  ├→ Higher level fish = higher selling price
  └→ More Cooking = fewer burns = more profit

Combat Training (Any combat skill)
  ├→ Kill enemies - drops loot + bones
  ├→ Loot → Sell to GE - profit
  ├→ Bones → Bury for Prayer XP
  ├→ Need Cooking food to sustain training
  └→ Create demand for Fishing → Cooking chain

Prayer Training (Bones buried)
  ├→ Source: Combat kills (must do combat first)
  ├→ Higher difficulty enemies = better bones
  ├→ Bones cost = Fishing → Cooking profit
  └→ Unlock prayer buffs for better combat
```

### Economic Principles (MVP LOCKED)

1. **No Single Path:** Players can specialize in Combat, Skilling, or balanced mix
2. **Interdependency:** Cooking enables Combat, Combat enables Prayer
3. **Profit Opportunities:** Each skill has profit potential at every level
4. **Free Market:** Players buy/sell via GE - price discovery organic
5. **Grindy Progression:** Level 92 = 50% XP, so endgame feels earned
6. **Tier-Based Gating:** Higher levels unlock better tools + gear
