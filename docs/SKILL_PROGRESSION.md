## 3. DEFENCE SKILL

**Purpose:** Reduces incoming damage, gates armor wearing, increases survival

### Armor Requirements & Protection

| Level | Helmet | Chestplate | Legs | Defence Bonus |
|-------|--------|-----------|------|---------------|
| 1 | Bronze full helm | Bronze platebody | Bronze platelegs | +5 |
| 10 | Iron full helm | Iron platebody | Iron platelegs | +10 |
| 20 | Steel full helm | Steel platebody | Steel platelegs | +20 |
| 30 | Black full helm | Black platebody | Black platelegs | +30 |
| 40 | Mithril full helm | Mithril platebody | Mithril platelegs | +40 |
| 50 | Adamant full helm | Adamant platebody | Adamant platelegs | +50 |
| 60 | Rune full helm | Rune platebody | Rune platelegs | +60 |
| 70 | Dragon full helm | Dragon platebody | Dragon platelegs | +70 |

### XP Gain Rate
- **Melee:** 1.33 XP per damage taken (passive)
- **Defensive stance:** 1.33 XP per damage dealt + Attack/Strength XP
- Slower than Attack/Strength but applies in all combat
- Cumulative: Level 99 = 13,034,431 XP

### Training Method (MVP)
- Passive: Train any combat style, gain Defence slowly
- Active: Use "Defensive" stance (select from combat menu)
  - Splits XP: 1.33 to Attack + 1.33 to Strength + 1.33 to Defence per damage
- Profit potential: Medium (combats enemies)

### Damage Reduction Formula
- Effective Defence = Defence level + Defence bonuses
- Incoming damage reduced by 10% per Defence level (rough estimate)
- Higher Defence = longer survival time

## 5. PRAYER SKILL

**Purpose:** Unlock prayers (buffs), gain XP by burying bones, manage prayer points

### Prayer System Mechanics

**Prayer Points:**
- Maximum = Prayer level (e.g., level 10 Prayer = 10 max PP)
- PP drain while active (variable by prayer, roughly 1-3 per tick)
- Restored at altars (full restore)
- Restored with Prayer potions (partial restore: Prayer_level × 0.25)

## ECONOMIC INTERCONNECTION (MVP)

### The Supply Chain

```
Woodcutting (Logs)
  ├→ Sell to GE (profit)
  ├→ Trade to Fletchings (future)
  └→ Use in Firemaking (future)

Fishing (Fish)
  ├→ Sell raw (profit)
  ├→ Use Cooking skill
  └→ Cooked fish heals in combat

Cooking (Food)
  ├→ Sell cooked fish (profit)
  ├→ Use for healing in combat
  └→ Required for long combat grinds

Combat Kills (Bones + Loot)
  ├→ Bury bones → Prayer XP
  ├→ Sell loot → gold
  └→ Drop bones for Prayer players to buy

Prayer (Trained via bones)
  ├→ Get prayers (buffs)
  ├→ Buffer combat effectiveness
  └→ Creates demand for bones
```

### Why This Matters
- **Woodcutting players** create logs → sell to GE → generates economy
- **Fishing players** create food → sell to combatants → fuels combat training
- **Combat players** generate bones → Prayer players buy them
- **All paths** feed the same economy
- **Free market** allows flipping at every stage
