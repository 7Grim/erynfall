## PART 2: PROGRESSION TIMELINE (Real OSRS Data)

### Level 1-10: Entry Phase

**Method 1: Training Dummy (Safest)**
- Time: 20-30 minutes
- XP gained: ~480-800 XP
- Gear: None required
- Profit: 0gp

**Method 2: Chickens**
- Time: 25 minutes
- XP per kill: ~12-15 XP
- Kills needed: ~40-60 kills
- Gear: Bronze sword (free)
- Profit: Low (feathers + bones)

**Method 3: Goblins**
- Time: 20-30 minutes
- XP per kill: ~20-24 XP
- Kills needed: ~25-40 kills
- Gear: Bronze sword
- Profit: Coins (20-50gp per kill)

**Reality:** Most players skip 1-10 with Training Dummy or Waterfall Quest (see below).

---

### Level 10-30: Rush Phase

**Method 1: Waterfall Quest (FASTEST)**
- Time: **10-20 minutes total**
- XP gained: ~4,620 XP (Attack + Strength to 30)
- Gear: Bronze sword + anti-poison potion
- Profit: 0gp (quest only)
- **Impact:** Saves ~2 hours of grinding

**Method 2: Manual Training (Cows/Goblins)**
- Time: **1.5-2 hours**
- XP rate: ~400-500 XP/minute
- Kills: ~200-300 total
- Gear progression: Bronze → Iron (level 5)
- Profit: 50-100gp

**Meta:** Most players do Waterfall Quest (10-20 min), then continue grinding from 30.

---

### Level 30-60: Grind Begins

**Training Spot: Rock Crabs (Most Popular)**
- Time: **10-15 hours** (30→60 for one skill)
- XP rate: ~40,000 XP/hour
- Gear: Iron (level 5) → Mithril (level 20) → Adamant (level 30)
- Progression feel: **First time grindy**, tedious but doable
- Profit: Negligible (bones + drops)

**Alternative: Flesh Crawlers**
- Time: **12-18 hours** (slower but safer HP training)
- XP rate: ~30,000 XP/hour
- Gear: Same progression
- Difference: Better Defence XP

**Milestone: Level 40 = RUNE ARMOR UNLOCKED**
- Massive gear upgrade
- Full Rune set costs ~145k gp
- Feels like major achievement
- Damage output increases noticeably

**Reality:** This phase takes **2-3 weeks** of casual play (10+ hrs/week).

---

### Level 60-85: The Real Grind

**Training Spot: Varies (Slayer, Rock Crabs, Experiments)**
- Time: **30-60 hours** (60→85)
- XP rate: ~30,000-50,000 XP/hour (depends on spot)
- Gear: Adamant/Rune/Early Dragon pieces
- Progression feel: **Noticeably grindy**, requires dedication
- Profit: Slayer tasks make it profitable (~500k gp/hour with drops)

**Why 60-85 Feels Different:**
- XP requirements double every ~7 levels
- Hit the 25% of total XP mark (3.3M out of 13.3M)
- Level 85 = significant prestige milestone
- Takes **1-2 months** of regular play

**Gear Upgrades:**
- Level 60: Dragon equipment unlocked (endgame aesthetic)
- Level 70: Fire Cape possible (PvM achievement)
- Level 75+: BiS weapons accessible

---

### Level 85-92: High Grind

**Training Spot: Nightmare Zone / Slayer**
- Time: **25-40 hours** (85→92)
- XP rate: ~50,000-60,000 XP/hour
- Gear: Dragon/Barrows pieces
- Progression feel: **Serious grind**, player commitment required
- Profit: High (100k+ gp/hour with BiS gear)

**Psychology Shift:**
- Players at 85 are "late-game" players
- Each level takes **4-5 hours**
- 25% to 50% XP mark reached (6.5M XP)
- Takes **2-3 months** steady play

**Context:** Level 92 = **50% of total XP to 99** achieved.

---

### Level 92-99: The Prestige Grind

**Training Spot: Slayer / AFK methods**
- Time: **40-60+ hours** (92→99)
- XP rate: ~40,000-50,000 XP/hour
- Gear: BiS gear (10M+ value)
- Progression feel: **Extreme dedication required**, not for casual players
- Profit: 500k+ gp/hour (justifies time investment)

**Psychology:**
- Level 99 = **Prestige badge**
- **Another 50% of total XP** (to 92) needed
- Players grinding 99 are hardcore
- Can take **3-6 months** of dedicated grinding
- Only ~5-10% of players reach 99

**Reality Check:**
- Level 92: "This player is serious"
- Level 99: "This player is hardcore/dedicated"

## PART 4: PACING PSYCHOLOGY (Why It Works)

### The Early Game Feel (Levels 1-40)

**Hours: 1-2 days of play (~20-50 hours)**

- **Fast level-ups:** Level 1 → 10 in 30 minutes feels AMAZING
- **Gear progression:** New sword/armor every 5 levels
- **New enemies accessible:** Rock Crabs at 20, Flesh Crawlers at 15
- **Motivation:** "What's next?"
- **Rewards:** Frequent sense of achievement

**Psychology:** Players feel progression happening. Addiction is high.

---

### The Mid-Game Grind (Levels 40-85)

**Hours: 100-400 hours of play (~2-6 months)**

- **Slowing curve:** 40→50 takes 2-4 hours, 50→60 takes 4-6 hours
- **Gear upgrades:** Less frequent (20 level gaps between upgrades)
- **New enemies:** Less often
- **New activities:** Slayer unlocks (optional progression)
- **Motivation:** "Just one more level..." / Prestige perception

**Psychology:** Grind is real, but players have unlocked "end-game" content. Switching between activities (Slayer, AFK training) keeps it fresh.

---

### The Endgame Grind (Levels 85-99)

**Hours: 400-1000+ hours of play (~6-12+ months)**

- **Extreme curve:** 85→90 = ~100 hours
- **Gear is maxed:** No new upgrades
- **Activities are known:** Slayer, Nightmare Zone, AFK training
- **Motivation:** Pure prestige / "I started this, I'm finishing it"
- **Badge of honor:** Visible achievement

**Psychology:** Players grinding 99 are intrinsically motivated. Not about gear or new content. About completing the ultimate challenge.

## PART 5: XP RATE FORMULA (Implementation Critical)

### Melee XP Calculation

```
XP per hit = 4 × (Damage dealt)
Total XP per kill = 4 × (Enemy HP)
```

**Example: Chicken (3 HP)**
```
Damage per hit: ~1-2 (level 1 player with bronze sword)
Hits to kill: 2-3
Total damage: 3 HP
XP earned: 4 × 3 = 12 XP per kill
```

**Example: Rock Crab (25 HP)**
```
Damage per hit: ~5-8 (level 30 player with adamant sword)
Hits to kill: 4-5
Total damage: 25 HP
XP earned: 4 × 25 = 100 XP per kill
Kills per minute: ~2-3 kills/min
XP per hour: ~40,000-50,000 XP/hour
```

### Enemy HP Scaling

This determines XP per kill, which directly affects progression speed.

**Rule:** Enemy HP should scale ~2× for every 10-15 level increase in combat level.

```
Level 5 enemies: 5-10 HP → 20-40 XP/kill
Level 15 enemies: 15-25 HP → 60-100 XP/kill
Level 30 enemies: 40-60 HP → 160-240 XP/kill
Level 60 enemies: 100-150 HP → 400-600 XP/kill
Level 99 bosses: 200+ HP → 800+ XP/kill
```

## CRITICAL IMPLEMENTATION NOTES

### For MVP:

1. **Early game must feel FAST**
   - Level 1-10 in 30 min on training dummy
   - Level 10-30 in ~2 hours with questing
   - Frequent gear upgrades (every 5 levels)

2. **Mid-game feels like grinding**
   - Still rewarding (3-5 hours per level, 30-60)
   - Gear upgrades every 10-15 levels
   - New training spots available

3. **Late-game is prestige**
   - 85-99 takes months
   - No new gear unlocks
   - Pure XP grind / self-imposed challenge

4. **Enemy HP scaling is crucial**
   - Affects XP per kill
   - Affects progression speed
   - Must scale with player level expectation

5. **Gear progression is motivation**
   - New sword = new damage = new enemies
   - Players farm for gear actively
   - "Just 5 more levels until Rune armor" (motivating factor)
