# OSRS-MMORP Project Discovery Questions

## Category 1: Project Identity & Goals (4 questions)

**Q1.1:** What is the primary purpose of this project?
- Is it a passion project, portfolio piece, commercial product, or learning exercise?

**Q1.2:** Who is the target audience?
- Friends/local play? Streamers/content creators? Casual players? Speedrunners? Mass market?

**Q1.3:** What is the long-term vision for this game?
- Is it meant to be a full OSRS-like experience, or a specific niche/twist on OSRS?

**Q1.4:** What does "success" look like?
- Is it: 10 friends playing? 100 concurrent players? Steam release? Streaming community? Revenue?

---

## Category 2: Scope & MVP (5 questions)

**Q2.1:** What is your MVP (Minimum Viable Product)?
- What's the smallest, playable version that's worth shipping?

**Q2.2:** How many concurrent players in MVP?
- Single-player offline? 4-player co-op? 10-100 simultaneous? 1000+?

**Q2.3:** Is multiplayer required for MVP, or is single-player acceptable?
- Should MVP be offline/singleplayer first, then add multiplayer later?

**Q2.4:** What gameplay systems are essential for MVP?
- Examples: Movement? Combat? Quests? Crafting? Economy? Leveling?
- What can wait for post-launch?

**Q2.5:** What's your timeline?
- 8 weeks? 6 months? 1 year? Open-ended?

---

## Category 3: Gameplay Mechanics (7 questions)

**Q3.1:** What is the core gameplay loop (minute-to-minute)?
- Example: "Pick up quest → Find objective → Kill mobs → Collect loot → Return to quest giver"
- Or: "Explore dungeon → Solve puzzles → Fight boss → Get reward"

**Q3.2:** What's the progression system?
- XP → Levels → Skills? Quests unlock content? Loot tiers? Something else?

**Q3.3:** Combat complexity?
- Simple: Click enemy, auto-attack until dead
- Medium: Click enemy, ability rotation (3-5 buttons)
- Complex: Positioning, cooldowns, AI prediction

**Q3.4:** Is PvP (player-vs-player) important?
- No PvP? Optional (opt-in servers)? Core mechanic (Rust-style)?
- If yes: How important? (5% or 50% of gameplay)

**Q3.5:** What's the core reward loop?
- Loot? Stats/levels? Cosmetics? Story? Social? Economy?

**Q3.6:** How does progression feel?
- Fast (noticeable improvement each session)? Slow (grindy)? Milestone-based?

**Q3.7:** Is there a story or narrative?
- No narrative? Light lore? Campaign-driven? Emergent storytelling?

## Category 4: Content & World (5 questions)

**Q4.1:** World scope?
- One small island (Tutorial Island size)? One continent? Multiple regions?

**Q4.2:** How many NPCs, quests, and locations in MVP?
- Examples: 5 NPCs / 3 quests / 10 locations? Or 50/20/50?

**Q4.3:** Is there a story/campaign?
- Linear story? Sandbox exploration? Mix?

**Q4.4:** How important are quests vs. free exploration?
- Is quest-driven progression required, or can players ignore quests?

**Q4.5:** What's the setting/theme?
- OSRS fantasy (medieval magic)? Dark fantasy (Condemned vibes)? Something else?

## Category 5: Technical & Constraints (7 questions)

**Q5.1:** How many hours/week can you realistically commit?
- 5? 10? 20? 30+?

**Q5.2:** Are you experienced with Java?
- Beginner? Intermediate? Expert?
- Is Java a comfortable choice, or should we consider alternatives?

**Q5.3:** Is cross-platform a requirement?
- Windows only? Windows + Mac + Linux?

**Q5.4:** Do you need network play immediately, or can MVP be offline?
- (Affects tech stack significantly)

**Q5.5:** Is rendering quality important early?
- Placeholder sprites acceptable for 3-6 months?
- Or does art need to be polished early?

**Q5.6:** Who's handling art/content creation?
- You solo? A dedicated artist? Outsourced? Placeholder-heavy approach?

**Q5.7:** Do you have a game artist joining, and if so, when?
- S1? S3? Post-launch?
